Background

Callsign: JENKO is an ongoing operation built around caffeine, questionable planning, and a refusal to sit still for too long. This site exists as a running log of training, time out bush, days on the road, and whatever side quests manage to derail the original plan.

Expect inconsistent updates, unnecessary purchases justified as "essential gear," and occasional moments of clarity between cups of coffee.

Mission Profile

Primary objectives include staying fit, getting outdoors, putting kilometres on the odometer, and documenting the process without overthinking it. Secondary objectives include testing gear, chasing good campsites, and pretending every weekend has a clear plan before it inevitably changes.

Mission scope remains flexible. Enthusiasm remains high.
